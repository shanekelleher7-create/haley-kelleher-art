About the Artist

Creating worlds you want to live in

I'm Haley Kelleher, and I make illustrations that feel like stepping into a story — warm rooms with something brewing on the stove, witches sipping tea with ravens on their chairs, pumpkin-headed figures wandering moonlit gardens, gnomes carrying mushrooms through earthy fields. Cozy, a little magical, always intimate.

My work is entirely hand-drawn using markers on paper. I'm drawn to the hidden details: a tiny gnome slipping through a mouse-door, soot sprites gathering under a bed, a worm tunneling beneath a terrarium world. The world I draw is populated with creatures and characters who exist just at the edge of what you'd normally notice.

I want my art to make you feel something warm and safe. Like you've just remembered a place you love, or a day that felt like magic.

Medium

Alcohol & water-based markers on paper and wood, with ink linework

Themes

Cozy interiors, witches, gnomes, pumpkin folk, quiet magic, cottagecore fantasy

Originals

Hand-drawn one-of-a-kind pieces, signed and shipped with care

Prints

High-quality fine art prints available in multiple sizes

Commissions

Your story, illustrated

Commission a one-of-a-kind original illustration from Haley — a cozy interior that feels like your own, a witch portrait with your own sense of magic, a pumpkin-headed figure in your favorite landscape. Each commission is thoughtfully crafted by hand with the same care and detail as every piece in the gallery.

01

Inquiry

Send a message with your vision, reference images, and approximate size or format.

02

Quote & Confirmation

Receive a quote based on size, complexity, and timeline. A deposit confirms your place in the queue.

03

Sketch Review

A pencil sketch is shared for your approval before full rendering begins.

04

Final Delivery

Your finished original is signed, photographed, packaged, and shipped with a certificate of authenticity.
